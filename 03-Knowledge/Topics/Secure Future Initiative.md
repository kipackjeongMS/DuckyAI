## Summary

Secure Future Initiative (SFI) is a Microsoft-wide engineering security program that drives improvements across authentication, infrastructure hardening, and supply chain security. SFI compliance requirements include removing hardcoded service references in pipelines, enforcing parameterized configurations, and reducing attack surface across deployment infrastructure.
